Get in Touch

Steps for Your Success

Our proven process takes you from idea to market-ready product

1

Pre-Study

We help you to further clarify your needs. Normally there are multiple needs, view points, feature requests, expectations and opinions within organizations and on the customer side. We make it easy for you!

Just nominate key contributors no matter where they physically are. We will interview the individuals, teams, key customers and form a further processed concept idea for you.

2

Competitor Benchmarking

We carry out solutions benchmarking as part of the process. You will get valuable insight for your competitor solutions, how those are built, what is missing, good inventions and what are the possible areas to be enhanced.

3

Requirements

Product or service requirements will form a picture about the final product. It describes features, use cases, environment, connections to related services and the target customer group.

This will help us and you to build a quality product that meets your, your team's, and your customer's expectations and needs while being ahead the competition.

This will be the basis for development work. Our agile working methods allow us to revisit the project requirements whenever there is mutually agreed need for it.

4

Development

Our Development process includes the following steps:

  • Graphical Design
  • User Interface Design
  • Functionality Design
  • Back processes and functions design
  • Development
  • Testing
  • Alpha and Beta releases (every 2 to 4 weeks until product meets its final agreed maturity)
5

Publishment

We publish the end product on your behalf (or assist you to become a publisher) in App Store, Google Play Store, Windows Phone Store.

6

Product Maintenance

Upon agreement, we can take care of all needed maintenance actions for you. This includes follow-up (statistics, download reports, user reviews and ratings, customer feedback) and a product roadmap proposal based on the customer feedback.

Web Applications / Cloud Solutions

Keywords for solutions we are currently developing are: Visual Studio, .NET, Microsoft Azure, C#, Java. Solutions are running as web applications so users need only web browsers to access and use the services. Installation can be either in Cloud environment or in customers' own private servers.

Technologies

Leading-Edge Technology Portfolio

We have applied many leading-edge technologies in our solutions. Our technology portfolio is expanding all the time.

Augmented Reality

Connects real and virtual objects in a real environment. Works interactively and in real time. We have been applying this technology since 2014.

This technology is based on image recognition using a smartphone/tablet camera. After the specified part of the image has been identified, the application can start multiple operations (animation, text, video, sound) on top of the actual identified image.

Possible use cases:

  • Industry – detect a machine and show maintenance instructions
  • Interior decoration – show 3D model of furniture in real dimensions
  • Product launch – show your product as a live 3D model
View Example on YouTube

Weather Service

Get weather information from the nearest weather station. We have been applying this technology since 2016.

Based on GPS positioning. The nearest weather station is called by sending location data to a service — you get temperature, air pressure, humidity etc. The service we utilize has more than 40,000 weather stations all over the world.

Possible use cases:

  • Modelling product behavior depending on environmental conditions
  • Assist in decision making where weather conditions need to be considered
  • House temperature control systems

Push Notifications

A push notification is a message that pops up on a mobile device. App publishers can send them at any time and users don't have to be in the app or using their devices to receive them. Sending Push Messages is free of charge.

Possible use cases:

  • Inform users about a new app release
  • Inform about the start of a competition
  • New product launch or important event notifications

User Identification

In solutions where the user is required to log in to the system, we use techniques which allow easy use and enough security. We have implemented support for Apple, Facebook and Google login solutions.

Our data is stored in servers located in Northern Europe and are thus under privacy policies of the European Union. We also have our privacy policy documented and attached into application descriptions in all app stores.

Location and Maps

Location can be sorted out in multiple ways and with solution dependent accuracy. Most location related solutions have a GPS based system in the background. Smart Phone devices can typically reach 4.9m accuracy in position.

We have used position information with Weather service, Ballistic calculations, OpenStreetMap and Google maps.

Possible use cases:

  • Navigators and nearest services finder
  • Safety solutions and tracking solutions

Mobile Payment

An application could offer many forms of digital content to be purchased. There are multiple payment methods supported: credit card, gift card, Operator billing. Each OS provider has their own payment service (App Store, Google Play Store, Windows Phone Store).

Possible use cases:

  • Sell advanced features for your product
  • Sell an annual license to use the application

Mobile Advertising

Mobile applications can be used to enable 3rd party advertising by enabling advertising channels. Advertisements can be banners, full page and with video content. With these channels, you can make additional income based on shown ads and ad clicks.

Campaigns can be profiled to show based on target country, language, demography, connection type etc.
