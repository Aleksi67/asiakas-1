Lapua Ballistics

Mobile Application • iOS & Android

Lapua Ballistics is designed to use 6DOF, the most precise ballistic calculation method. It runs in Android and iOS operating systems for smartphones and tablets. Special care and attention has been put into usability and versatile needs of the calculator.

You can easily define your own weapon and select latest Lapua cartridges and bullets from built in library that is automatically updated if new data is available. Define your weather information manually or upload it from your local weather station.

Based on the information, Lapua Ballistics will calculate the trajectory information for you. The calculation results are shown on numbers, sight picture, tables and graphs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to change Unit Values on Lapua Ballistics? +
There are many ways to change unit values in Lapua Ballistics application. Different cases are explained in the Lapua Ballistics User info document, Pages 7, 10 and 14.
Are you able to enter left Twist Rate? +
Yes, give the twist rate with – value. For example -9.5.
Are you able to enter negative Coriolis Value? +
Yes, + for north value and – for south Coriolis value.

Vihtavuori Reload

Mobile Application • iOS & Android

Vihtavuori Reload is a free of charge mobile application to help you with reloading process as well as to keep track of your reloading recipes. Easy recipe management and creation. Latest Vihtavuori Reloading Data access.

Frequently Asked Questions

I can't login to VihtaVuori RELOAD +
If you see "Invalid email address" error, check your email address. Sometimes the text editor in Android devices leaves additional space at the end of the email address, especially if you select the "auto" routine to copy the email. Please check that there is no extra empty space at the end of your email address.
Is the FB login and email login going to same account? +
No. Both options create their own accounts. FB login and E-mail login do not harmonize data between each other.
